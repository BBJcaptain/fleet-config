Fleet Collector

Offline · Keyless
Jot down an aircraft's configuration offline, then Download collected .json and merge it into the fleet later with the editor.
Safe to use in flight. This page has no signing key, no password, and no fleet data. It never connects to the internet and does no encryption, so it works fully offline. It cannot publish to the fleet — only the private editor on the ground can do that.
Resume a collection you saved earlier.
Empty. Tap Add aircraft to collect and fill in what you see on the airframe.
Fill in the Registration first — that is how the editor matches your notes to the right airframe when merging (an unknown registration is added as a new aircraft). Leave a field blank if you did not check it; blank fields are not merged, so they will not overwrite existing data. Dropdown values match the app exactly; free-text boxes remember your own entries on this device.
Your work is saved automatically on this device after every change, so it survives closing the app, a reload, or the phone reclaiming memory in flight — the green “Saved” stamp shows the last save. It reloads by itself next time you open the page. When you are back on the ground, tap Download collected .json, merge it in the editor, then tap Clear draft to start clean.
